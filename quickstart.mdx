## What you'll do in this guide

By the end of this 5-minute walkthrough, you'll have:

1. The SiteZen plugin installed on your WordPress site
2. Your WordPress site connected to the SiteZen platform
3. Your Figma access token saved
4. Your first Figma section converted and live on WordPress

<Callout kind="info" collapsed="false">
  Already done some steps? Jump straight to [your first conversion](/getting-started/first-conversion).
</Callout>

## Before you start

Make sure you have:

- A WordPress site you can install plugins on (admin access)
- A Figma account with the file you want to convert
- A SiteZen platform account ([sign up here](https://sitezen-platform.vercel.app))

## Step 1: Install the SiteZen plugin

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download the plugin zip" icon="download" title-type="p">
    Get the latest `sitezen-plugin.zip` from your SiteZen account dashboard.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Upload to WordPress" icon="upload" title-type="p">
    In WP Admin → **Plugins → Add New → Upload Plugin** → choose `sitezen-plugin.zip` → **Install Now**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Activate" icon="check" title-type="p">
    Click **Activate** on the install success screen.
  </Step>
</Steps>

[SCREENSHOT: WordPress plugin upload page with sitezen-plugin.zip selected]

Detailed guide: [Installing the plugin](/getting-started/installing-plugin)

## Step 2: Get your connection key

After activating the plugin, WordPress will show a **SiteZen** menu item in your admin sidebar.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open SiteZen settings" icon="settings" title-type="p">
    WP Admin → **SiteZen → Settings**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Copy the Connection Key" icon="copy" title-type="p">
    A unique key was auto-generated on activation. Copy it.
  </Step>
</Steps>

[SCREENSHOT: SiteZen Settings page showing the connection key with a Copy button]

## Step 3: Connect WordPress to the platform

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the SiteZen platform" icon="external-link" title-type="p">
    Go to [sitezen-platform.vercel.app](https://sitezen-platform.vercel.app) and sign in.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add your WordPress site" icon="plus" title-type="p">
    Click **Sites → Add Site**. Enter:
    - **Site URL**: your WordPress URL (e.g. `https://yoursite.com`)
    - **Connection Key**: the key you copied from WP Admin
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save and verify" icon="check" title-type="p">
    Click **Save**. The platform tests the connection — you'll see a green checkmark if successful.
  </Step>
</Steps>

[SCREENSHOT: Platform Sites page with new site form]

Detailed guide: [Connecting plugin to platform](/getting-started/connect-plugin)

## Step 4: Add your Figma access token

SiteZen needs read access to your Figma files to extract text, colors, and images.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Generate a Figma token" icon="key" title-type="p">
    Go to [figma.com/settings](https://www.figma.com/settings) → **Personal Access Tokens** → **Generate new token**. Name it "SiteZen" and copy the token.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Paste it in SiteZen" icon="clipboard" title-type="p">
    Back in the platform → **Convert** page → paste the token in the **Figma access token** field.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The token is stored locally in your browser only — never sent to our servers.

Detailed guide: [Getting your Figma access token](/getting-started/figma-token)

## Step 5: Convert your first section

<Steps>
  <Step title="Copy a section URL from Figma" icon="link" title-type="p">
    In Figma, right-click the section frame you want to convert → **Copy link to selection**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Paste into SiteZen" icon="clipboard" title-type="p">
    On the **Convert** page, paste the URL into **Figma section URL**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Upload a screenshot" icon="image" title-type="p">
    Take a screenshot of just that section in Figma (Cmd/Ctrl+Shift+4 or use Figma's export). Drop it into the **Section screenshot** area.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Pick where it lands" icon="map-pin" title-type="p">
    Choose **Page** (new WordPress page) for your first try. Leave **New page** selected.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Convert" icon="zap" title-type="p">
    Click **Convert this section**. Wait ~30-60 seconds.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Push to WordPress" icon="upload" title-type="p">
    When the preview loads, click **Push to WordPress** in the top right.
  </Step>
</Steps>

[SCREENSHOT: Convert page filled out with all fields]

Detailed guide: [Your first conversion](/getting-started/first-conversion)

Click the WordPress page URL that appears in the push success notice. You'll see your Figma design — live, responsive, and editable.

## Troubleshooting

**Plugin won't activate?** See [Plugin installation issues](/troubleshooting/plugin-activation).

**Conversion timed out?** See [Conversion errors](/troubleshooting/conversion-errors).

**Site connection failed?** Make sure your WordPress site is publicly reachable and the connection key is pasted exactly (no spaces).
