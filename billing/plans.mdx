## What's included in every plan

All paid plans include:

- Unlimited WordPress sites you can connect (within site limit)
- Full access to all block types (Tabs, Pricing, Forms, etc.)
- All template types (Header, Footer, Tab, CPT Single, etc.)
- Frame detection (batch conversion)
- Fix Issues retries (each counts as a section)
- History view + re-push
- Email support

## Which plan is right for you

### Pick Starter ($39/mo) if:
- You're a freelance designer or solo agency
- You ship 1-2 small sites per month
- You're testing SiteZen for your workflow
- You manage 3 or fewer client WordPress sites

55 sections covers ~11 pages, or 5-10 small landing pages per month.

### Pick Studio ($99/mo) if:
- You're a small agency (2-5 team members)
- You ship 4-8 client projects per month
- You manage 5-10 active WordPress sites
- You need faster support response (24h vs 48h)

165 sections covers ~33 pages, or 3-5 mid-size sites monthly.

### Pick Agency ($279/mo) if:
- You're a mid-to-large agency (5+ team members)
- You ship 10+ client projects per month
- You manage unlimited WordPress sites
- You need priority queue (faster API processing during peak)
- You need fastest support (8h response)

470 sections covers ~94 pages, or 10-15 sites monthly.

## What counts as a "section"

A section is **one conversion** (one click of "Convert this section" on the platform).

| Action | Counts as |
|---|---|
| Convert a Figma section + push to Page | 1 section |
| Use Fix Issues on a preview | 1 section (each retry) |
| Convert + push to Template | 1 section |
| Convert + push to CPT | 1 section |
| Re-push existing history conversion to a different site | 0 sections (no new conversion) |
| Edit a SiteZen Section block directly in WordPress | 0 sections (no API call) |
| Failed conversion (engine error) | 0 sections (no charge for our failure) |

## Overage pricing

Hit your cap before the month ends? Two options:

### Option 1: Buy overage credits
- Per-section rate based on your tier (see table above)
- Charged at end-of-month with your subscription invoice
- Auto-stops at the cap you set (default: $50 max overage)

### Option 2: Upgrade your plan mid-cycle
- Upgrade now → immediate access to new tier's cap
- Prorated charge for remainder of current cycle
- Renewal at new plan's full price next cycle

## Annual billing

Pay yearly upfront, save:
- **Starter annual**: $421.20 (saves $46.80, = 10% off)
- **Studio annual**: $1,009.80 (saves $178.20, = 15% off)
- **Agency annual**: $2,678.40 (saves $669.60, = 20% off)

Switch monthly → annual anytime in **Settings → Billing**.

## Unused sections rollover

Yes — unused sections **carry forward to the next month** indefinitely while you have an active subscription.

Example:
- Month 1: Studio plan (165 sections), used 100 → 65 carry forward
- Month 2: starts with 165 + 65 = 230 sections available
- Used 200 → 30 carry forward
- Month 3: starts with 165 + 30 = 195

If you cancel: accumulated rollover credits expire at end of your last paid month.

## Free trial

New accounts get **20 free sections** to test before paying. No credit card required.

After trial:
- Pick a plan to continue
- Or sit dormant with no plan — your account stays, but conversions are blocked until subscribing

## Team members

Currently, each subscription is **single-user**. Multi-seat for agencies on the roadmap.

Workarounds for teams:
- Share the same account login among 2-3 team members (no charge difference)
- Use Agency plan for larger teams (higher cap absorbs multi-user usage)

## Changing plans

**Upgrade**: takes effect immediately. Prorated charge for remainder of cycle.

**Downgrade**: takes effect at next renewal date. You keep current tier benefits until then.

**Cancel**: subscription stops at end of current paid month. You retain access until then. Accumulated rollover credits expire on cancellation date.

## Refunds

- **First 14 days**: full refund, no questions asked
- **After 14 days**: pro-rated refund for unused portion of monthly subscription
- **Annual plans**: pro-rated refund for unused months

Contact support → request refund.

## Common questions

**Q: Can I switch plans mid-month?**
Yes — upgrade is immediate (prorated), downgrade kicks in next cycle.

**Q: Do failed conversions count?**
No — engine errors are not charged.

**Q: What if I hit cap mid-conversion?**
The conversion completes (won't cut you off mid-run). The overage starts billing on the NEXT conversion after cap.

**Q: Can I pause my subscription?**
Annual plans only — pause up to 30 days, no charge during pause. Monthly plans: just cancel and resubscribe.

**Q: Is there a one-time pack option (no subscription)?**
Not currently. Subscription model only. Annual billing approximates a one-time pack for budget purposes.
